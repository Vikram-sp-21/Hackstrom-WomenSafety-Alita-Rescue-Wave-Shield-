About This Platform

Alita Rescue Wave Shield is an AI-powered mobile app that enhances women's safety by providing safer route suggestions, smart alerts, voice-activated SOS, and emergency contact integration, delivering timely help during emergencies. It also offers offline AI chatbot guidance for safety tips and first aid.

Fully inspired by past SIH (SafeScape, RakshaNet), this platform demonstrates actionable AI-powered safety, and a clear social impact.
